BRAND OF THE MONTH

Featured Brand · May 2025

Druk Lab

Born in the shadow of the Himalayas, Druk Lab blends ancient Bhutanese textile traditions with cutting-edge streetwear silhouettes. Founded by a collective of young designers from Thimphu, the brand has become synonymous with bold dragon-motif prints and quality-first construction. Their signature "Druk" hoodie has become the most-wanted piece in the country.
