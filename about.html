The Origin

Born From
The Streets of
Thimphu

Urban Drip was born at the crossroads of tradition and rebellion. Founded in Thimphu, Bhutan, we believe that streetwear is not just clothing — it is a statement, a culture, a way of being in the world.

Our curated works connect the traditional and the innovative, presented to those who have their own way of walking. Every drop is a conversation between the ancient wisdom of Bhutan and the relentless energy of global street culture.

We are not just any store — we are a portal. A gateway between Bhutanese identity and the world beyond the mountains.

"Our mission is to introduce the most iconic streetwear brands in the world to Bhutan and to export the Bhutan Fashionist vision to the world."

We believe there are no limits to fashion, and we are here to bridge the gap between Bhutanese dreamers, creators, and fighters with the world of street-wear. We are not just any store — we are a portal.
